Welcome to the Metanoia Curriculum for educators

A Journey of Transformation

Welcome to the Metanoia curriculum, designed to guide students through a transformative learning journey about plastic waste challenges and solutions. As an educator, you play a crucial role in facilitating this process of discovery, analysis, creativity, and action.

This dashboard provides quick access to all the resources you need to implement the curriculum effectively. Explore the modules, implementation guides, and supplementary materials to make this journey meaningful for your students.

Uganda Context

  • Approximately 600 tons of plastic waste generated daily in Kampala
  • About 6% of plastic waste is properly recycled
  • National ban on kaveera bags with varied enforcement
  • Waste pickers provide ~80% of recycled material

South Africa Context

  • Approximately 2.4 million tons of plastic waste annually
  • Recycling rates around 14%
  • Plastic bag levy and Extended Producer Responsibility regulations
  • About 60,000 waste pickers recover 80-90% of recycled packaging
